	LiquidCrystal::leftToRight


	void LiquidCrystal::leftToRight();

This sets the LCD to display text left to right (think English).

Usage:

	LiquidCrystal lcd(8, 9, 4, 5, 6, 7);
	lcd.begin(16, 2);
	lcd.leftToRight();
